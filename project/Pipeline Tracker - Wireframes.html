Two views, one toggle

A top nav flips between the Dashboard (read-only health of the pipeline + commission earnings) and the Loan Database (the working table where loans are entered & edited).

Commission math is automatic

2% at close + 0.5% monthly trail for the life of the loan. Never typed in — always derived from Loan Amount & Close Date so the numbers can't drift.

★ Your column ask

You weren't sure which columns you'll keep. So columns are rename-able and removable. A handful of core data points stay locked — Loan Amount, Status, Close/End Date and the auto commissions — so trimming the table never breaks the money math. See the Manage columns ★ frame.

Manage columns ★

Your big ask. Show only what you need. Drag to reorder, toggle to hide, ✎ to rename, × to remove an optional column entirely. Core data points are locked (green) — they can't be renamed away or removed, so the commission math is always safe.

Why lock some?

If someone deleted “Loan Amount” or “Close Date”, the 2% / 0.5% / months-active numbers would break. Locking just those keeps you free to trim everything else.
